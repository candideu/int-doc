Patricia "Trish" is an Industrial Designer and graduate student at OCAD University of Kenyan descent. She speaks, reads, and writes in English and Swahili. She also understands Kikuyu, her mother's traditional language. She mainly thinks in English, but thinks in Swahili when she is speaking Swahili, and uses English the most. Trish is Candide's classmate, Roy's friend, and Salome and Mwenda's daughter.
